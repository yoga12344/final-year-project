### What the Demo Covers

1. **System startup** – docker-compose bringing up all services
2. **Real-time trust scoring** – LSTM processing OpenStack log stream
3. **DQN policy adaptation** – watch `policy.json` update live during an
   attack simulation (user "mallory" triggers anomaly → DENY + isolation)
4. **Federated Learning round** – multiple simulated clients aggregating
   model updates through the Flower server
5. **Blockchain audit trail** – querying the Fabric ledger for immutable
   decision records
6. **Dashboard visualisations** – ROC curve, trust score timeline,
   decision distribution charts
